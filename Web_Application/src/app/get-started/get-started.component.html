ABOUT FRESTY

Welcome to the Fresty website. Fresty helps you to distinguish good fresh fruits and vegetables from the bad ones. Yet, with the busy lifestyles, inspecting and buying fresh fruits and vegetables has made a tedious process to the public. Moreover, the suppliers and retailers don’t quality grade their products. Lack of proper quality grading devices is one of the major reasons. Moreover fresh fruits and vegetables won’t undergo appropriate quality grading process before those are sold to customers. To sort out this issue Fresty helps you to quality grade the Fruits and vegetables. Through this web application the user can view the history of the quality grading results that they have done. Yet, a user can only get this service if they logged into the system through the mobile application. Furthermore, the user also needs to login to the web application to view the history of quality grading results. Currently this quality grading system will work for limited fruits and vegetables.

FRESTY Mobile App








The image in left side is the first view of the Fresty mobile app. The user needs to log into the mobile application to quality grade the fruits and vegetables. Or the user can use the application as a guest user. Once the user logged in, they should take a photo and the image will automatically get uploaded to the system. Then the system will recognize whether it's fresh fruits or vegetables in the uploaded image. Also, bounding boxes will be created around the recognized fresh fruits or vegetables in the image. Features of those recognized fresh fruits or vegetables are extracted as the next step. Then the features are analyzed and given the output of the quality grading process through this Mobile application.
